ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat) Services

ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat) Services offer diagnosis and treatment for conditions related to the ear, nose, throat, including hearing loss, sinus issues, and balance disorders.

  • Removal of foreign bodies from ENT areas.
  • Audiometry & hearing loss management.

  • Microscopic ear & throat surgeries.

  • Advanced sinus endoscopy with laser & Coblation.
